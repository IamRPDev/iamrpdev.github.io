# Commands
Standard Chezmoi commands for managing the environment.

### Substrate Modifications
Commands are wrapped in Substrate-aware wrappers (e.g., `cz-commit`, `cz-discover-aliases`) that integrate with local AI inference (phi4-mini) and UCH policy checks.

### Applications
Used for daily lifecycle management of the fleet: `llmadmin01` (Master) and `t430` (Hypervisor).
